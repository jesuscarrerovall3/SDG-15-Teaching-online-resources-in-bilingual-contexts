Learn About SDG 15 🌍

🌳 Forests

Forests are called the lungs of the Earth because they produce oxygen. They help clean the air and provide homes for millions of animals.

🪓 Deforestation

Deforestation means cutting down too many trees. This destroys animal habitats and increases climate change.

🦁 Endangered Animals

Some animals are endangered because humans destroy habitats, pollute nature, or hunt illegally.

🐝 Pollinators

Bees and butterflies help plants grow by moving pollen from flower to flower. Without pollinators, many foods would disappear.

💧 Clean Water

Rivers and lakes are important ecosystems. Animals, plants, and humans need clean water to survive.

♻️ Recycling

Recycling helps reduce pollution and protects forests by reducing the need to cut down trees for paper.

🌱 Planting Trees

Trees absorb carbon dioxide and help fight climate change. Planting trees also creates homes for wildlife.

🐘 Wildlife Protection

Wildlife protection means keeping animals safe from hunting, pollution, and habitat destruction.

🌍 Ecosystems

An ecosystem is a community of living things and their environment. Forests, deserts, rivers, and oceans are ecosystems.

🔥 Wildfires

Wildfires can destroy forests and animal habitats. Some fires happen naturally, but many are caused by humans.

🚯 Pollution

Pollution harms animals and ecosystems. Plastic waste is especially dangerous for wildlife.

🌾 Sustainable Farming

Sustainable farming protects soil and nature while growing food. Farmers can grow crops without harming ecosystems.

🌸 Plants Matter

Plants give us food, medicine, oxygen, and materials. They are essential for life on Earth.

🐢 Habitat Loss

Habitat loss happens when forests or natural areas disappear. Animals may lose their homes and food sources.

☀️ Climate Change

Climate change affects forests, animals, and weather patterns. Rising temperatures can damage ecosystems.

🧹 Keeping Nature Clean

Picking up litter and reducing waste help protect parks, rivers, forests, and wildlife.

🦜 Rainforests

Rainforests contain more than half of the world’s plant and animal species. They are extremely important ecosystems.

🐺 Food Chains

Animals and plants depend on each other in food chains. If one species disappears, the ecosystem can change.

🌎 Protecting Earth

Everyone can help the Earth by recycling, saving water, reducing waste, and respecting nature.

👧 Kids Can Help!

Children can make a difference by planting trees, learning about nature, and teaching others.
